My studies

I'm currently in my second year of a Computer Science degree at the University of Lens (62300), where I'm studying application development. My interest in computer science began in Première at the André Malraux high school in Béthune (62400) when I chose the Numerical and Computer Science option (NSI) in addition to the Engineering Science and Mathematics options in a General Bac with the Euro English option. During this course, I later decided to stop taking the Mathematics option and concentrate on computer science and engineering.

I then did a distance learning immersion course at the University of Lens in the second year of my DUT, which confirmed my choice of further study and my desire to go to this school. After going through the Parcoursup application process, I was finally accepted at the IUT de Lens - Application Development option.
